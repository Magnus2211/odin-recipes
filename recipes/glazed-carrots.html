Glazed Carrots

HMMM,this looks like a tasty dish.Let's see what we will need to create this dish

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds carrots,peeled and cut into sticks
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground white pepper

Directions

Step 1

Place carrots into a large saucepan;poyr in enough water to reach depth of 1 inch and bring to boil. Reduce heat to low,cover,and simmer carrots until tender,8 to 10 minutes.Drain and transfer to a bowl.

Step 2

Melt butter in thr same saucepan over low heat.Stir in brown sugar,salt and white pepper until dissolved.Add carrots to the saucepan and toss until coated in the brown sugar mixture.Cook and stir until carrots are heated through and fully coated with glaze,1 to 3 more minutes.
